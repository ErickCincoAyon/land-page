Welcome to ZAKURA X

Zakura X is 9,999 art pieces drawn by Anime artist Desi and individually chosen by the Zakura X team! Zakura X lives in the Ethereum Block chain after an incident in her hometown of Osaka, Japan left her trapped in the Metaverse. Zakura X NFTs are 9,999 different moments in her travels throughout the metaverse each one giving their holders history of her past and future. Owning a Zakura X grants you membership to Zakura X private channels and holder only perks!

FAQ

  • What is Zakura X?

    Zakura X is a collection of 9,999 unique Anime Style NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain. All traits are hand-drawn by anime artist Desi.

  • Why was Zakura X created?

    Zakura X was created with the goal of bring the world of Anime into the world of Blockchain with partnerships and unique utility unseen in the NFT space.

  • Where can you Mint a Zakura X?

    Minting will be healed on the Zakura X website or directly through the smart contract. Smart contract, time, and date of release will be announced on our discord and updated on the website.

  • How long after mint will the Zakura X NFT's be revealed?

    Zakura X is a collection of 9,999 unique Anime Style NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain. The reveal will be 48hours after all Zakura NFTs are minted.

  • What Blockchain is Zakura X on?

    Zakura X is an ERC-721 token on the Ethereum Blockchain file hosting is IPFS.

  • How many Zakura X will the team mint?

    59 Zakura X will be minted for the team, giveaways, and marketing. The team will also be adding to a community fund where more Zakura X NFT's will be added for giveaways and marketing.
